# Northwind Analytics — Company Overview

Northwind Analytics is a fictional internal analytics platform company.

The company builds internal tools for business teams that need searchable reports, operational dashboards, and document-grounded assistants.

The company has three major internal products:

1. InsightBoard
   A dashboard and reporting product for managers.

2. DocuPilot
   A document question-answering assistant for policies, runbooks, and project notes.

3. SignalWatch
   A monitoring and alerting product for operational metrics.

The company’s main engineering principle is:

"Make the system explainable before making it clever."

This means every AI-generated answer should be traceable to the source document whenever possible.
